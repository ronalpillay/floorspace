# Product

## Users
High-end corporate clients, facility managers, enterprise decision-makers, and architects seeking premium, turnkey interior contracting services.

## Brand Personality
Premium, minimalist, editorial, confident, architectural. We speak softly but carry immense expertise. The brand feels like a high-end physical architectural magazine like Poliform.

## Anti-References
- Generic SaaS startup websites.
- Bubbly, friendly "tech" aesthetics.
- Template-driven corporate designs with cheap stock photos.
- Pure blacks and icy whites (we use warm, rich tones instead).

## Strategic Principles
- Let the architecture breathe. Use asymmetric, editorial bento-box layouts.
- Text is treated as typography, not just copy. Every word earns its place.
- Animations must be buttery smooth, purposeful, and never jarring.
